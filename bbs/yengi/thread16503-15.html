IOP Publishing: New editorial management system
Having trouble viewing this e-mail? View it in your web browser.

IOP Publishing


Dear Dr Yikmat,

We are making some changes to the way that we manage submissions and the peer review process.   Between May and November 2013 we will be implementing a journal-by-journal change as we move from our in-house submission and peer review management system (ATOM) to ScholarOne Manuscripts.

There will be a transition period as new submissions will be processed using ScholarOne Manuscripts  and existing articles will be processed using ATOM.  We very much appreciate your support during this time and we will do all we can to minimise the disruption.

Further information on the migration, including a rough timetable of when each journal will switch over to the new system, is available here.

The decision to move from our existing system to ScholarOne came in response to a significant growth in submissions. The number of articles we publish has almost doubled in recent years and that number is expected to continue to grow. We have selected ScholarOne to support our growth and to provide our authors and referees with the best possible experience during the peer-review process.




What are the benefits to me as an author or referee?

•Improved workflow which will lead to a reduction in average publication times
•Referee and author requests will include deep links within the e-mail so there is no need to remember a username and password
•Improved international access
•ScholarOne is a familiar interface used by many other journal publishers, which is simple and intuitive to use


Contact us

If you would like further information about the move to ScholarOne, please contact us at custserv@iop.org or tweet us at @IOPscience.

Best wishes,
Nicola Gulley
Editorial Director
IOP Publishing
custserv@iop.org

مەن مۇشۇ نىيوتون دىگەن بالاڭزىنىڭ « يەر شارىنىڭ تارتىش كۈچى » دىگەن قانۇنىغا قارشى دەڭلا،
نىمىشقا دىسەڭلار ؛ شۇ زاماندا نىيوتون ئالما دەرىخىنىڭ يېنىدا ئولتۇرۇپ ، بىر تال ئالمىنىڭ پوككىدە يەرگە چۈشكىنىنى كۈرۈپلا يەر شارىنىڭ تارتىش كۈچىنىڭ تەسىرىدىن چۈشۈپ كەتتى ، دەپ يەكۈن چىقارغان .
دىمىسىمۇ ئالما دىگەن پىشىپ ۋايىغا يەتكەندە ياكى ئىچىدىن قۇرۇتلاپ كەتكەندە يەرگە چۈشۈپ كەتمەي ئاسمانغا چىقىپ كەتسە بۇلامتى ، ئۆزىگە تۇشلۇق ئېغىرلىقى  بولغاندىكىن ئەلبەتتە يەرگە چۈشىدۇ-دە .
