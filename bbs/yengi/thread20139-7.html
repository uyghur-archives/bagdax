Sanubar Tursun performs in London

Delicate & sensuous vocals from the heart of Central Asia

Sanubar Tursun

Date: 12 May 2014Time: 7:00 PM

Finishes: 12 May 2014 Time: 9:00 PM

Venue: Brunei Gallery Room: Brunei Gallery Lecture Theatre

Type of Event: Concert
Series: SOAS Concert Series
Sanubar Tursun is one of the finest singers in Central Asia today; a star performer regarded by many as a symbol of the Uyghur nation. Her delicate and sensuous vocals breathe fresh life into the classical Muqam repertoire as well as traditional songs. She is accompanied by her brothers, including the virtuoso satar player Hesenjan Tursun, on this, her first European tour.

Presented in collaboration with the Aga Khan Music Initiative, a program of the Aga Khan Trust for Culture.
