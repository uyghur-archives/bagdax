Special Issue On: Performance Analysis of Public vs. Private Sector with Special Reference to Asia

Submission Due Date
09/23/2014
  
Dear Sir/Madam,



Greetings from International Journal of Productivity Management and Assessment Technologies (IJPMAT), USA !!!!!!!!!!!

Researchers and practitioners are invited to submit papers for this special theme issue on Performance Analysis of Public vs. Private Sector with special reference to Asia.


Introduction
The theme covers Public and Private Sector across all the Industries in Asia. This is becoming a hot topic in countries like India, China, SAARC countries, and various other nations across Asia.

Recommended Topics
Topics to be discussed in this special issue include (but are not limited to) the following:

Ø  ·         Education

Ø  ·         Performance analysis of state banks vs. private banks

Ø  ·         Performance analysis of state owned healthcare facilities vs. private sector

Ø  ·         Performance analysis of state owned telecommunications and private owned

Ø  ·         Performance analysis of state universities vs. private universities

Ø  ·         Performance analysis of public transportation vs. private transportation

Ø  ·         Power

Ø  ·         Privatization and efficiency

Ø  ·         Purchasing performance: comparison of public vs. private sector

Ø  ·         Real estate

Ø  ·         Retail

Ø  ·         Skill development

Ø  ·         Technology

Ø  ·         Textiles and apparel

Ø  ·         Tourism and hospitality

Ø  ·         Transport Infrastructure

Ø  ·         Urban development

Ø  ·         Urban infrastructure



ABOUT IJPMAT

The International Journal of Productivity Management and Assessment Technologies (IJPMAT) is an Official Publication of the Information Resources Management Association, Hershey, PA 17033-1240, USA. It is published by IGI Global. IGI Global, headquartered in Hershey, Pennsylvania (USA), is a leading international academic publisher of more than 2,300+ reference books, 155+ journals, encyclopedias, teaching cases, proceedings, and databases focusing on the areas of: education, social science, library science, healthcare, business, environmental science, public administration, computer science, and engineering.

The International Journal of Productivity Management and Assessment Technologies (IJPMAT) publishes new research, innovations, developments, and ideas in the areas of productivity, efficiency, and performance measurement and management, including new techniques, approaches, and related reflective analysis designed to improve individual, group, and organizational performance. The journal provides an interactive and global discussion forum for academics, professionals, and practitioners working and interested in the research and practice of productivity, efficiency, and performance measurement and management. This multidisciplinary journal draws from the fields of economics, econometrics, management, statistics, production, information technology, marketing, accounting, supply chain, and organizational behavior.


Submission Procedure

Researchers and practitioners are invited to submit papers for this special theme issue on Performance Analysis of Public vs. Private Sector with special reference to Asia on or before Sep. 23, 2014. All submissions must be original and may not be under review by another publication. Interested Authors should consult the Journal’s Guidelines for Manuscript Submissions at http://www.igi-global.com/Files/ ... inessubmission.pdf. All submitted papers will be reviewed on a double-blind, peer review basis. Papers must follow APA style for reference citations.

We shall be thankful, if you could circulate this information among your colleagues and motivate them to submit papers.
