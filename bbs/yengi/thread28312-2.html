CALL FOR PAPERS

INTERNATIONAL JOURNAL OF GLOBAL MANAGEMENT (ISSN: 0975-8291)

Indexed in EBSCO Directories

VOl. 5 Issue 1

NO PUBLICATION CHARGES.


Faculty of Management, VNS Group of Institutions is one of the leading management institutes in central India. The Institute has been ranked consistently in the upper edge of top ranking business schools in the nation-wide surveys carried out by the agencies like Business Today, Cosmode, Ministry of Human Resource Development, Business India and Outlook.

International Journal of Global Management (IJGM) is a Bi-Annual print journal of Faculty of Management, VNS Group of Institutions with ISSN 0975-8291. It aims to provide a forum for interdisciplinary discussion and attempts to serve as a bridge between the scholar and the practitioner. The Journal is indexed in prestigious EBSCO Directory, being published since 2010.

The Journal calls paper for publication in Vol.5, Issue 1.

The objective of the journal is to provide forum of discussion of advancements in the area of management, Information technology and International business. The journal publishes research papers, articles, book reviews and case studies. The journal invites manuscripts on all aspects of management and business environment. The journal publishes articles from the areas such as finance, accounting, marketing, operations management, human resource management, industrial relations, statistics, international business, information technology, environment, risk management, globalization and related areas. Articles reflecting diversity, cross-functional nature of management and emerging concerns are also considered for publication.

The journal follows a systematic review process. The research articles submitted, goes through pre-review screening by the editorial board for its significance and quality. The article which is found to be appropriate is only forwarded for blind-review to the subject experts. This review process may take around four months. During review process, contributor(s) may be asked to amend and re-submit the research article as per the comments of expert/reviewer. After this, the final article will be included for its possible publication in the journal.

The author can submit the soft copy of manuscript anytime in M.S. Word format after preparing the same as per our submission guidelines, at the email address – vnsijgm@gmail.com.

Submitted   papers   should   not   have   been    previously   published   nor  be   currently  under consideration for publication elsewhere.

In case of any query, please feel free to contact at email address –

vnsijgm@gmail.com

Publication in the month of APRIL 2015.  

Regards


Dr. Pratyush Tripathi
